About

Hi, I'm Shahzeb Qazi. I'm a Toronto-based software engineer and computer science graduate. My technical focus revolves around software architecture using pure functional paradigms in Haskell (web apps, CLIs, CI/CD), and building local AI inference systems optimized for high-shared-memory hardware architectures (64GB+). When I'm not configuring Linux window managers or managing infrastructure, I'm developing 2D fighting games in Godot, playing bass and guitar, or engineering audio for my band.
